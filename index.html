ABOUT

Hi, I'm Fruzsina.

A product designer with 4 years experience based in Malmö, originally from Hungary. Currently developing myself and the amazing products at Graphisoft.

But here's the twist – before diving into design, I spent 4.5 years climbing the QA ladder, going from trainee to Staff QA Engineer. Being a Test Manager and Test Analyst, I learned the ins and outs of the development process. This QA background helps me talk the talk with developers and stay a step ahead in the design game.

Oh, and did I mention I have a master's in Architecture? Juggling complex projects is when I'm in my element, especially the during brainstorming part. I thrive on collaboration and bringing ideas to life.

So, if you're looking for a designer who's not afraid of complexity, loves tossing around ideas, and can chat tech with developers, that's me. Let's make something awesome together!
